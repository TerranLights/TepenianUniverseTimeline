# Michelle Stanton — Conflict Experiences

Following the Long Night War, Michelle chose to take up residence in Concordia and help maintain the city's infrastructure. Her subsequent work turned toward data archaeology and verification — investigating the Great Corruption of Janbogo's pre-Falkland Treaty archives, and whether it was accidental or a deliberate, targeted erasure. She traces the shape of what is missing from the record as evidence in itself, working in parallel with Vosora Lashár Tanslock, who pursues the data-recovery side of the same inquiry.
