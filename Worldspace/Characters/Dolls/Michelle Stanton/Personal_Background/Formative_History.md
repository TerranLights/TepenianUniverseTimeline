# Michelle Stanton — Formative History

Michelle was built as a construction robot in Midwestland and was exiled to Tepenia following the signing of the Falkland Treaty. She became a leading figure in the laying and construction of the infrastructure for what became the Antarctican internet, later known as the Arcanet. Before the Long Night War, she divided her time between occasionally stopping in various nearby cities to rest and braving the frozen wastelands of Antarctica in a scientifically/technologically advanced Rastra while building Arcanet infrastructure.
