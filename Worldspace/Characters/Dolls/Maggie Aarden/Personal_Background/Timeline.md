## Post-Activation / Pre-Falkland Treaty
* Shortly after the International Court of Diplomacy's ruling at Jeju-Do: began working as an industrial yard worker in Midwestland, maintaining military craft (jets, tanks, etc.).
* Developed a love for Steampunk and began creating her own art outside of work.
* Was eventually let go from her industrial position as anti-robot sentiment rose across the world; became a very unwilling leader of her people, tasked with leading them out of primarily human cities to establish their own separate, parallel communities in Midwestland and the Colorado Republic.

## Falkland Treaty Era (2564 onward)
* Her pre-war resettlement effort was underway when the War of Upper Earth broke out; the war's escalating anti-robot violence turned her small effort into a refuge and rallying point for displaced robots, making her a recognized, credible leader by the war's end.
* Signed the Falkland Treaty at the city of Stanley, Falkland Islands, as the robot population's signatory — pressured into it by Upper Earth's Assembly Powers via a false "invitation to volunteer."
