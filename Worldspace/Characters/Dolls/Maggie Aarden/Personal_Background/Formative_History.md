# Maggie Aarden — Formative History

From Midwestland, shortly after the official decision by the International Court of Diplomacy at Jeju-Do. Initially began working as an industrial yard worker, maintaining the workings of Midwestlander military craft such as jets, tanks, etc. Later developed a love for Steampunk and began creating her own art outside of work.

Was eventually let go, and anti-robot sentiment began rising across the world. Became a very unwilling leader of her people, forced by circumstance very much against her will into a position of responsibility for her fellow robots. Was to lead them out of primarily human cities to establish their own separate, parallel town in Midwestland and the Colorado Republic.
