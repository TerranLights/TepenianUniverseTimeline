# Maggie Aarden — Loyalties & Beliefs

* Primary loyalties (faction, district, ideals, self)
    * To her own private interior world and craft, first and foremost by preference — she would rather be left alone with her expertise than be responsible for anyone else.
    * To her community, by an inescapable sense of duty that overrides her own preference for withdrawal — she cannot fully abandon people who need her.
* Conflicts in loyalty
    * Her signing of the Falkland Treaty: pressured into it by Upper Earth's own governments (the Assembly Powers), not by her own robot community, precisely because her established wartime credibility as a leader made her signature valuable to the victors — she became the face of an exile she never wanted to negotiate, imposed on her people by the side that had just defeated them.
