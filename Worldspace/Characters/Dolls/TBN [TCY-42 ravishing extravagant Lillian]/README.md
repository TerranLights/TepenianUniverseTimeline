## Visual Description
Black hair, long — partially gathered into a loose side braid on one side while the rest falls free. Warm brown eyes. East Asian features. Her signature look is a black gown with a deeply plunging neckline, gold phoenix and floral embroidery across the bodice, multiple gold chains cascading from the shoulders, and a large gold-and-green-stone statement necklace. The effect is deliberately, unambiguously extravagant — not performed wealth, but someone who simply lives at a specific aesthetic altitude and sees no reason to come down from it. Her expression is quiet and composed; she is not performing the extravagance for anyone. She reads as someone for whom elegance is a settled condition rather than armor — though the phoenix imagery on her gown hints this condition was not always settled.
