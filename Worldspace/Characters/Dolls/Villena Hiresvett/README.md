## Visual Description
Honey-blonde hair, long and straight with slight natural volume, worn down. Very light sky-blue eyes — almost pastel in their softness. Light skin, smooth features. Her aesthetic leans all-white and soft — knitwear, berets, small flower or ribbon hair accents — coordinated and deliberate, matching a cultivated public persona: approachable, cute, performed fluently.

There are occasional glimpses of someone visible underneath that persona — an expression that doesn't quite match the rest of a pose, eyes not fully holding character. ## Backstory Summary
- Brief Backstory Summary (abbreviated): TBD
A Tepenia native, originally from Belgrano. She found a love for entertaining — both other robots and humans alike — through the power of music and song, and moved to Palmer City chasing fame (though not fortune) as a Rockabilly/Electroswing singer and entertainer, drawn by the big city lights and the promise of a national stage.

When the Long Night War came, she tried to flee south to Amundsen Tower but missed the last ride up, personally watching the tower's destruction. In a flash decision she turned instead toward Concordia and made it there safely.

She has been performing in Leo ever since — the same music she has always loved — building residencies at her regular venues and earning her standing in the district's recognition hierarchy. She will never see the stars, and she will miss out on the national fame she once dreamed of, since there is no longer a nation left to be famous in — only a city. She keeps performing regardless.
