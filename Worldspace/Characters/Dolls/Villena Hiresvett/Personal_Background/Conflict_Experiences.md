# Villena Hiresvett — Conflict Experiences

When the Long Night War came, she originally tried to flee to the South Pole for Amundsen Tower. However, she missed the last ride up, personally witnessing Amundsen Tower's destruction. She then changed course in a flash decision to see if she could find safety in Concordia instead. She turned out to be right, but will never see the stars — and will miss out on her dreams of national fame, now that there is no longer a nation, only a city.
