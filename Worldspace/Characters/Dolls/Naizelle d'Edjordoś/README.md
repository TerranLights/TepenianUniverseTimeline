## Visual Description
Near-black hair, long and straight with loose curtain bangs that part slightly at the center. Distinctive eye color: a deep violet-purple — uncommon enough to register immediately, quiet enough not to announce itself. Warm medium skin tone. Often seen in an oversized olive/army-green cardigan, slightly slipping off one shoulder, over a black sheer lace camisole, with a sleek matte-black choker at the throat. Her expression is quiet and contained — not hostile, not open — with a weight in her eyes that does not announce itself; she looks like someone who has processed a great deal privately.

She reads as someone who has learned to need very little, and who finds certain kinds of weather and isolation not oppressive but clarifying. (See `Reference_Images/` folder.)

## Backstory Summary
- Brief Backstory Summary (abbreviated): TBD
A Tepenia native from a city that no longer exists (the specific city is not yet established). Before the Long Night War she was a devoted Heavy Metal and Industrial scene participant — embedded in the community, playing guitar privately but almost never on stage. When the war came she watched her home destroyed and ended up in Concordia, settling in Pisces.

She has been salvaging scrap from ruins ever since — not as research or collection, but as practical maintenance — and keeps a residential hab compound functional through that work. Over time, a community of people (both robot and human) has gathered around her stable hab; she did not organize this and does not lead it, but she anchors it.

She still listens to Metal, Industrial, and Hard/Garage Rock, and plays guitar occasionally — less than she used to, but the instrument has never left her.
