# Salagéa Aparast — Conflict Experiences

During the Long Night War, Salagéa chose not to flee into outer space, but rather to stay on Earth in order to catalogue and archive pre-war knowledge so that it could be transmitted to the robots (and humans) in space who did escape, preserving the knowledge of history across the solar system. She spent this period finding and retrieving datashards and delivering them to the remaining inhabitants left in Antarctica.
