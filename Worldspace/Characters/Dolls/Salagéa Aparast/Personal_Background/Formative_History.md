# Salagéa Aparast — Formative History

A Tepenian native, originally from the city of Belgrano. She lives specifically on a boat which she sails on her own — not "huge" in any traditional sense, but reasonably large enough to live aboard.

She makes her livelihood by receiving datashards from the old pre-war internet and then delivering them throughout Tepenia so that the information and human knowledge from the old pre-war internet can be logged and archived via the Arcanet. She works tirelessly to preserve the backlog of civilizational knowledge so that it is never forgotten, all the while evading hostility from humans of Upper Earth.
