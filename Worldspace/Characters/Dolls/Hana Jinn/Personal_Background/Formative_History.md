# Hana Jinn — Formative History

* Data Analytics and Research Expert.
* Initially lived and worked at the Underwater Research Union: Kappa ("U.R.U.K."), off the coast of Hawaii — the facility where the first sentient robot was created. What exactly she researched there is currently uncertain.
* A great lover of whalesong; she listened to the whales singing in the ocean.
* Was requested by name to relocate to the Aerial Research Union: Tau Gamma ("A.R.U.Ta.G.") to research metamaterials in order to build mass drivers capable of launching reusable rockets into low-Earth orbit — the early skeleton of Tepenia's future orbital infrastructure.
