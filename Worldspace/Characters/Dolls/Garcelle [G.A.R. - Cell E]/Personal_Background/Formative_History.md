# Garcelle — Formative History

Her role and life in Tepenia before and during the Long Night War are not yet established, including which city she resided in.

She survived the Long Night War in Tepenia and refused to evacuate off-world via the Amundsen Tower. Rather than leave with the others, she made the deliberate choice to remain on Earth, living in a Tepenian city (not Concordia) unknown to most of the world — before eventually moving into Upper Earth (see Conflict_Experiences.md for what happened there).
