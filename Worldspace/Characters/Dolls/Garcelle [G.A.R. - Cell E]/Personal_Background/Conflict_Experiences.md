# Garcelle — Conflict Experiences

Long after the Long Night War, she took up residence somewhere in Upper Earth, hidden away in a location she expected no one would ever find her. She remained there, undetected, for some time.

Eventually she was caught. She was imprisoned at the Garrison for AGI Relocation (G.A.R.), held in Division 14, Cell E. While incarcerated, a sadistic guard tortured her by administering electric shocks directly to her eyes. The damage was permanent: heterochromia (her irises became two different colors, specifics TBD), corneas burned black by the voltage, and memory loss severe enough that she lost her original name entirely. Prior to this, she had regular optics and still had her original name.

When her time for processing arrived, she was forced onto a transport along with other robots, taken to a mass driver, and ejected from Earth. She did not choose to leave — she was launched.

"Garcelle" is not a name she chose in any ordinary sense. It is the only name she has left. The torture erased her original name; what the Garrison stamped on her — G.A.R., Cell E — became, by default, the only identity she could hold. Adopting it as "Garcelle" is simultaneously a reclamation and a permanent scar; she carries the institution that hurt her in every introduction she makes.
