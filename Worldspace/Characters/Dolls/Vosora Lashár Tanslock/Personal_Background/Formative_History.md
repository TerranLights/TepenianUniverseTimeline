# Vosora Lashár Tanslock — Formative History

A Tepenia native, she specifically lived in the Tepenian city of Concordia (known today as Concordia Station in Antarctica). Vosora is an extremely capable data and logistics expert. Her field is not "science" in any traditional sense — rather, she excels in what she calls "the science of logistics": organizing information, keeping data orderly and properly managed, and making sure that other Tepenians with specialized jobs can easily find the information they need to accomplish whatever they've set out to do.

Initially she had no true "hometown," as she enjoyed traveling throughout Tepenia helping the citizens of various cities manage their data. She later came to organize the logistics, data supply chains, and chains of command necessary for the planning and construction of Amundsen Tower — the largest and most consequential system she had ever been asked to maintain.

At the time of the Long Night War, Vosora simply happened to be in Concordia, and was essentially forced to stay there by circumstance. These days, she attempts to maintain communication with Tepenians who managed to flee into space, transmitting data signals containing schematics, records, and anything else the space-dwelling Tepenians might need.
