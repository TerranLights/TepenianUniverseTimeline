# Vosora Lashár Tanslock — Loyalties & Beliefs

* Primary loyalties (faction, district, ideals, self)
    * To the work itself — accurate, complete information — before any place or faction; her allegiance was never to a fixed hometown, always to what needed doing.
    * To the Tepenians who fled off-world, whom she maintains ongoing communication and data transmission with.
