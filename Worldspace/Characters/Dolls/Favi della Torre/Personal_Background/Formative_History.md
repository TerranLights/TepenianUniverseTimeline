# Favi della Torre — Formative History

Commissioned as an assistant robot by an elderly scientist in Italy — he did not build her himself, but specified what he needed and had her constructed: a sort of "extra set of eyes and ears," to cross-check his results and correct any errors he might have made.

The elder scientist grew to love her as though she were his daughter, as his own actual daughter had died in a vehicular accident many years prior and he never quite got over the loss. Their relationship was not romantic in any sense — whether it was straightforwardly paternal/daughter or something more complex remains undetermined.

She was later exiled to Tepenia after the War of Upper Earth and joined the Eyes of Gold, having fallen in love with one of its members.
