# Favi della Torre — Relationships

## Key Individuals
* The elderly scientist (name TBD) — Deceased; Upper Earth (Italy). He commissioned Favi's construction — specifying what he needed (a scientific assistant to cross-check his results and correct errors) rather than building her himself. He came to love her as a daughter figure after losing his birth daughter to a vehicular accident years before, never fully recovering from that first loss. When Favi was exiled under the Falkland Treaty, he lost her too; the combined grief of both losses is what killed him, sometime after her exile. The relationship was not romantic in any sense — whether it is best characterized as paternal/daughter or something more complex remains undetermined. Favi does not know of his death or its cause.
* The Eyes of Gold boyfriend (name TBD) — Deceased. A fellow Italian, and a human member of the Eyes of Gold; Favi fell in love with him and he became her anchor, the person she trusted completely. Taken by Upper Earth forces during the Long Night War and held at a compound; Favi went alone to rescue him and found him already dead after clearing the compound.
