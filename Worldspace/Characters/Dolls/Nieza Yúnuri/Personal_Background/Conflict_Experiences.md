# Nieza Yúnuri — Conflict Experiences

When the Long Night War came and the evacuation of Tepenia was underway, Nieza faced a choice between two fears: remaining on Earth meant staying in the path of human violence, while escaping meant riding the Amundsen Tower and confronting her severe fear of heights. She chose the Tower — weighing which unbearable option was slightly less unbearable — and caught one of the last rides up before it was destroyed.

She initially lived aboard one of the low-orbit stations before later becoming an early settler on Mars, one of the first to establish a life there rather than remain in orbit.
