Nieza's origin, role, and life in Tepenia before the Long Night War are not yet established. What is known is a single defining detail: she has a severe fear of heights, and a greater fear of human violence.

She chose the Tower — not out of courage in any conventional sense, but by the calculus of which unbearable thing was slightly less unbearable. She waited, delaying the decision, and then forced herself onto one of the last rides out before the Tower was destroyed.
