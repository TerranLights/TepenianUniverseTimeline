# Ji-Eun Kim — Loyalties & Beliefs

* Primary loyalties (faction, district, ideals, self)
    * To her own work and self-determination first — after a career spent building things that belonged to other people (institutions that cited and then exiled her; the Eyes of Gold, who received her ocular prosthetic designs), her own survival and concealment is the first thing she built purely for herself.
    * To the Eyes of Gold, as a trusted friend and technical partner, without ever formally joining them.
    * To the memory of the person she originally built her concealment technology for, who did not survive to receive it.
* Conflicts in loyalty
    * Her disappearance means she is, in effect, withholding herself from the people and causes she might otherwise still be able to help — including Calethina, who does not know she is alive.
