# Ji-Eun Kim — Formative History

Her early life and existence took place during a time when, despite robots having legal personhood rights, global anti-robot sentiment was at an all-time high among humans due to a variety of incidents. She became a nanotech engineer who had to fight tooth and nail to earn respect for her competency and work her way to the top of her field. By the late 25th century, she had become known as one of the greatest and best nanotechnicians on the planet.
