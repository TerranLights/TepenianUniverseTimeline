## Post-Activation / Pre-Falkland Treaty
* Built and trained in Unified Korea, during a period of all-time-high global anti-robot sentiment; fought her way to the top of the nanotech engineering field.
* By the late 25th century: recognized as one of the greatest nanotechnicians on the planet — a global, not merely Tepenian, reputation.

## Falkland Treaty Era (2564 onward)
* June 21, 2564: Exiled to Antarctica following the Falkland Treaty, along with the broader robot population. First landing at Sejong (King George Island, Palmer Subnet).
* Relocated to Zhongshan (Larsemann Hills, Mirny Subnet).
* Later moved to Concordia; established a nanotech testing facility and continued her research; engineered the Eyes of Gold's gold ocular prosthetics.

## Long Night / Midnight War
* Disappeared before or during the Long Night War — not as a casualty, but by deliberate choice, using her own nanotech expertise to make herself permanently undetectable.

## Post-Exile / Tepenia Era (Current)
