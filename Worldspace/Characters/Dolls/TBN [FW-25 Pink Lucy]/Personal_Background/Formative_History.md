# TBN [FW-25 Pink Lucy] — Formative History

Originally from Dumont d'Urville (Adélie Land, East Antarctica) — a Tepenia native. **Resolved 2026-07-12:** before the Long Night War, she relocated from Dumont d'Urville to Janbogo (via Highway 183), settling there — not to Casey, the route's previously-considered alternative. She did not flee directly from Dumont d'Urville to a final destination; she had already moved on before the war. Janbogo's civic identity (a city whose whole character is about making people feel at home, absorbing and re-originating whatever culture reaches it) closely matches her own established personality, and was the deciding factor over Casey, whose more muted, function-oriented civic character was a weaker fit, and which was in any case destroyed early in the war as a targeted strategic chokepoint. Whether she subsequently moved on to Concordia or remained in Janbogo through the war is still open.
