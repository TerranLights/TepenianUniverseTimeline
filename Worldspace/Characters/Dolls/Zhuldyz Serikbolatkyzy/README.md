## Backstory Summary
Zhuldyz was built to work as a farm hand and assistant for the Serikbolat family in Kazakhstan. The family patriarch spent his entire life savings to purchase her for his son, Turar Serikbolat, who was to inherit the family land and property, as there would be no other surviving children to work the farm alongside him. She developed expertise in farm equipment — tractors, cutters, plowers, and their maintenance mechanics.

Turar and Zhuldyz fell deeply, madly in love and dedicated themselves to each other. When the War of Upper Earth came, they escaped to Antarctica together. In Tepenia, Zhuldyz applied her mechanical skills to salvaging equipment. What happened to Turar during the Tepenia years and the Long Night War is not yet established.

When the Long Night War came, Zhuldyz escaped via the Amundsen Tower. She initially lived aboard an O'Neill Cylinder, cutting up derelict spaceships and selling scrap to pay rent on her hab unit. She later moved to Mars, where she runs her own scrapyard today.
