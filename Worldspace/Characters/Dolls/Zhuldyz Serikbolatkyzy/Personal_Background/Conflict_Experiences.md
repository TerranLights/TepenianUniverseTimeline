# Zhuldyz Serikbolatkyzy — Conflict Experiences

When the War of Upper Earth came, Zhuldyz and Turar Serikbolat escaped to Antarctica together. In Tepenia, Zhuldyz put her mechanic skills to use salvaging equipment.

When the Long Night War came, Zhuldyz escaped via the Amundsen Tower and later put her skills to use once again, cutting up derelict spaceships and selling the scrap for money to pay rent for the hab unit inside the O'Neill Cylinder she lived aboard. She later moved to Mars, where she continues to live today and runs her own scrapyard.
