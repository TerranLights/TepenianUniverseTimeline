# Zhuldyz Serikbolatkyzy — Formative History

Zhuldyz was originally built to work as a helping hand and assistant on a farm in Kazakhstan. The patriarch of the Serikbolat family spent his entire life savings to buy her for his son, Turar Serikbolat, as he was to inherit the land and property, and there would be no other surviving children to work the farm. Zhuldyz began working on the mechanics of farm equipment — tractors, cutters, plowers, and the like.

Turar and Zhuldyz fell madly, deeply in love and dedicated themselves to each other. Soon afterward, the War of Upper Earth came, and Turar and Zhuldyz escaped to Antarctica together.
