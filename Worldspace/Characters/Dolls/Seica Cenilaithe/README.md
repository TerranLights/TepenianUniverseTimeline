## Visual Description
Dark brown hair, not black, with lighter auburn highlights, medium length and worn loosely flowing. Clear, vivid blue eyes that read cold against her warm skin tone and dark hair. Fair-to-medium skin, slightly flushed at the cheeks. Often seen in a white Victorian-inspired lace blouse — delicate, embroidered at the shoulders, slightly sheer, modest at the neckline — occasionally paired with black theatrical ram horns as a striking, deliberately theatrical contrast between softness and gothic imagery.

Her expression is neither cold nor warm — direct and controlled, force held at a specific, chosen level, with significant power underneath that is not so much concealed as governed. She reads as someone whose visual presentation says "I am not fragile," while the white dress says "I was soft once." Both are simultaneously true, and she knows it. ## Backstory Summary
- Brief Backstory Summary (abbreviated): TBD
A Goth robot and Tepenia native. Once had a human husband whom she loved deeply — they loved each other very deeply. He was killed during the Long Night War. She carries a burning hatred against Upper Earth governments and militaries for taking him from her; she would not direct this against innocent citizens and has no way of reaching the actual responsible parties, so it lives inside her.

She came to Concordia and settled in Scorpio, embedded in its Goth community — the faction that believes transformation should be sacred rather than clinical, that death should be honored rather than therapized. Her art and her spiritual practice are the primary channels for what she carries. She is also notably skilled with a one-handed ballistic pistol, a separate and specific skill distinct from her artistic and spiritual life.
