# Ayako Hayashi — Formative History

Initial origins currently uncertain, though she is confirmed to be from Japan; what she was originally built/activated for has yet to be established. She went through a period of severe depression. She later discovered the philosophy of Arthur Schopenhauer and turned to art as a way to release and integrate her psyche, finding that she had a love for art and fashion for its own sake.

Later, after being forcibly exiled to Antarctica following the Falkland Treaty, she lost the human man who was the love of her life to accidental causes. This inspired her to learn First Aid and basic field medicine in an attempt to make sure she never let any innocent person die again. It is currently uncertain whether she was the founder of the Red Spiral or simply a joining member.
