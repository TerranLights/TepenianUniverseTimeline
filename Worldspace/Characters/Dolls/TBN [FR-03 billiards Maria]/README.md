## Visual Description
Long, very straight blonde hair and vivid blue eyes. Her signature look is a black sheer mesh dress with a keyhole neckline, a small black bow/necktie collar at the throat, a black bikini bra visible underneath through the sheer fabric, and fishnet side panels. She is consistently pictured at a billiards table with a cue in hand, green felt behind her. Her expression is slightly open-mouthed and faintly teasing — someone who noticed she'd caught your attention and is deciding whether to be amused by it. She reads as someone who chose this game, this room, and this outfit on purpose; the billiards table functions as her territory. (See `Reference_Images/` folder.)
