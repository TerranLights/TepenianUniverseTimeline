## Visual Description
Warm honey-gold hair, long and loose — not styled for anyone, just how it falls, with natural volume and slight wave. Grey-blue eyes, slightly heavy-lidded, with a quality of looking without quite focusing on what's in front of her — not vacant, watching, but inward; a slight moodiness lives in the eye shape. Full lips, soft features, a small beauty mark visible in close reference shots. Full-figured and soft. She wears a delicate chain necklace with a small pendant, a smartwatch-style bracelet, and in some references a small toe ring — nothing decorative in an expensive sense, just things she keeps on because she always has.

Her signature at-home clothing is a white floral halter dress with small red flowers scattered across it — light, short, clearly domestic; this is how she dresses when she's only dressing for herself, not for going out. Her expression across reference material is consistently quiet — not performing warmth, not cold, waiting for something she hasn't named. Her hands are often held together or resting on herself: self-contained, slightly uneasy with being observed. The clearest reference image shows her seated, chin resting on her fist, not looking at anything in particular — someone who has spent a great deal of time sitting with her own thoughts, not in distress, but not at peace either. The setting around her in reference material — a curated sideboard, floral lace tablecloth, tea cup, birdcage lamp — suggests a home that is warm, privately arranged, and cared for without announcement.
