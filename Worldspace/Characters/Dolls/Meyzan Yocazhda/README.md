## Visual Description
Jet-black hair in a high messy bun with straight fringe bangs falling forward across her forehead. Her defining feature is her eyes — vivid cobalt blue, almost uncanny in their color saturation, immediately arresting — accented by a small star-shaped jewel sticker just below the outer corner of one eye. She favors gold heart-drop earrings with stone insets and a purple velvet choker with a silver ring pendant. Her presentation is composed and staged — someone comfortable being observed because she chose every element of what is being observed; nothing about it reads as accidental.
