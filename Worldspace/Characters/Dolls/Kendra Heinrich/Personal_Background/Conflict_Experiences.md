# Kendra Heinrich — Conflict Experiences

During the final evacuations before Amundsen Tower's destruction, Kendra held the line at the base of the Tower with an SMG and ballistic weapons, standing between Upper Earth military forces and the innocents making the last desperate rides up before the line closed.

The Upper Earth strike that ultimately ended her stand did not target her or the evacuees directly — it struck the base accelerator/power complex, the single point that kept the tower's structure standing. Once that power was cut, the tower's structure lost its support and came down over the following several minutes, the bulk of the mass landing centered almost exactly on the position she was holding. She was not defeated by an enemy she failed to stop, but by the collapse of the tower itself.

She is presumed to have survived by being caught near or inside the underground foundation shaft rather than the open surface, which was structurally separate from the collapsing tower above and plausibly remained largely intact even as the surface was buried. Injured, and with her way out sealed under a mountain of wreckage, she has been stranded there ever since.
