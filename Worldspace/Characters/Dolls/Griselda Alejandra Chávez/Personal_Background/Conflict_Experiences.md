# Griselda Alejandra Chávez — Conflict Experiences

When the Long Night War came and the evacuation of Tepenia began, Griselda secured one of the earlier rides up the Amundsen Tower into low-Earth orbit — she did not wait for the last flights. She initially lived aboard one of the early low-orbit stations before later joining one of the expeditions to Mars, where she established a scrapyard and trading business on Deimos that she continues to run today.

Palmer City — the city whose performance culture her instruments had fed for years, via the Belgrano-Esperanza-Palmer City trade route — was destroyed in the Long Night War, and most of its population did not survive. Griselda herself was never a resident of Palmer City or even its subnet; that distance from Belgrano may be the reason she survived at all.
