# Griselda Alejandra Chávez — Formative History

Griselda was originally built as a blacksmith robot in the Republic of Sonora — the Arizona desert region of post-2083-war "New North America." She was exiled under the Falkland Treaty's blanket provision, though not immediately — she left for Tepenia only once news of the treaty reached Sonora, some time after its June 21, 2564 signing.

She settled and set up her smithy in Belgrano (Halley subnet, Coats Land/Weddell Sea coast) — her city of residence in Tepenia, not her origin — using her blacksmithing skills to craft musical instruments, specializing in pianos. She developed a personal signature of using whalebone in place of wood for her instruments; she did not cut or shape the whalebone herself, sourcing it already cut and shaped from someone else (identity unestablished), but incorporated it into the pianos and other instruments she built alongside her own metalwork.

She shipped finished instruments by sea across the Weddell Sea from Belgrano to Esperanza (the one Palmer subnet city on the Antarctic Peninsula mainland, itself Weddell Sea-facing), then onward via highway to Palmer City, feeding its musical and performance culture. She used her knowledge of metalwork and repairs to keep her own physical body under constant maintenance.
