## Backstory Summary
- Brief Backstory Summary (abbreviated): TBD
Griselda was built in the Republic of Sonora as a blacksmith. The Falkland Treaty's blanket exile provision applied to her the moment it was signed on June 21, 2564, but word didn't reach Sonora immediately; she arrived in Tepenia some time after the treaty, once news of it had made its way to the Arizona desert.

She settled and set up her smithy in Belgrano (Halley subnet, Coats Land/Weddell Sea coast). Rather than continue purely as a blacksmith, she applied her metalworking skills to craft musical instruments, specializing in pianos, with a personal signature of using whalebone in place of wood for the instruments' structural/body components (sourced already cut and shaped from an unnamed supplier). Finished instruments traveled by ship across the Weddell Sea from Belgrano to Esperanza, then via highway to Palmer City, feeding directly into its performance culture. She also used her metalworking knowledge to keep her own frame under constant self-maintenance.

When the Long Night War came, Griselda secured one of the earlier rides up the Amundsen Tower into low-Earth orbit — she did not wait for the last flights. She initially lived aboard one of the early low-orbit stations, later joined an expedition to Mars, and established a scrapyard and trading business on Deimos, which she continues to run today.

Palmer City was destroyed in the Long Night War; most of its population did not survive. The handful of Palmer City survivors who made it to Concordia are the primary source of any knowledge about Griselda — she was not famous herself, but a craftsperson who made instruments for people who were.
