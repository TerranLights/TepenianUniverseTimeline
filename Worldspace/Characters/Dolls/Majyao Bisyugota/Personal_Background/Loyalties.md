# Majyao Bisyugota — Loyalties & Beliefs

* Primary loyalties (faction, district, ideals, self)
    * To her patrons and to the idea of the teahouse as a sanctuary — she is not a political creature and does not belong to any faction; her loyalty is to the people who walk through her door.
* Conflicts in loyalty
    * (Not yet established in source material — she maintains deliberate neutrality, and whether that is conscious policy or simply a byproduct of loving everyone who visits is left as an intentional ambiguity.)
