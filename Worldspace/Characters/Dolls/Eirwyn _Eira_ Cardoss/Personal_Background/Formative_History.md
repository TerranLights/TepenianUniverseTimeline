# Eirwyn Cardoss — Formative History

She is an iceblade assassin. She believes in the cause of preserving the old pre-war internet archived in the Arcanet, and in the sovereignty of robots as paramount above all and anything else. She has no qualms about "removing" any human who tries to harm a robot, and will kill — efficiently, silently — without a second thought to defend the interests of her nation.
