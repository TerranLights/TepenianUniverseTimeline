# Eirwyn Cardoss — Conflict Experiences

Following the War of Upper Earth, she regularly went into Upper Earth lands to eliminate any human who posed a threat to Tepenia, as well as retrieving anything that could have been valuable to her people. At one point, she was finally caught and imprisoned.

The human man who served as one of the guards over the cell-block that her cell was located in eventually came to agree with her cause, fell in love with Eira, and helped her escape. In doing so, he effectively became even worse than a "human Upper-Earth exilee" — he became classified as a traitor. He and Eira went on to defend Tepenian interests together.

Due to the passage of time, he died — possibly while on assignment with Eira, possibly of old age; undetermined — before the second war (the Long Night War), though he absolutely would have fought alongside Tepenians if he'd been alive at the time.
