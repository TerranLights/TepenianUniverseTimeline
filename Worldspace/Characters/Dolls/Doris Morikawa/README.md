## Backstory Summary
- Brief Backstory Summary (abbreviated): TBD
Doris Morikawa served as Head Librarian at the Hall of Archives in the Sovereign Republic of Hawaii — a location described as a politically and economically neutral zone for scientific research. She was responsible for the cataloging and preservation of much of pre-war earthly knowledge, maintained against the possibility of a global catastrophe.

She succeeded in that responsibility. What became of the archives — and of her — when catastrophe came in the form of the War of Upper Earth and the Long Night War is not yet established. Whether the archives survived, were partially preserved, or were lost is a significant open question.

She is not known for any dramatic act of heroism; she is known for having cared, systematically and completely, about the survival of human knowledge. If any of the Hall of Archives' records survived and reached Tepenia — through evacuees, data transfers, or physical media — her name would be on them, representing a pre-digital, pre-Arcanet, human-scale curation effort distinct from (and possibly complementary or contradictory to) what the Arcanet itself holds.
