# Trisha Miller — Formative History

A radio DJ host originally from Midwestland. She played the sort of rock 'n' roll music that was played on the radio in the 1980s during the mornings and evenings when people would listen on their car radio during their commute to or from work.

She lived during a time when human-robot tensions were at a then-all-time high, and so there were many people throughout New North America who were against her — but there were also people who supported and loved her.
