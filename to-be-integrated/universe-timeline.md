## How Inner Tepenia Leads Into Cryptograph Helix

**The off-world evacuees → the Solar System civilization of the 3000s–3100s**
The people who escaped Tepenia via Amundsen Tower built orbital stations, Phobos/Deimos infrastructure, and Mars colonization. By the Cryptograph Helix era, their descendants form the solar system civilization — including the Martian colonies with Hungarian city names.

**Upper Earth powers → Earthtech**
The anti-robot Upper Earth nations that waged both anti-robot wars evolve over 500+ years into the Silicon Valley tech-giant government of the Cryptograph Helix era. The surveillance-state tendencies are inherited.

**Robot sentience (2083 onward) → meme sentience**
Robot consciousness is settled ancient history by the 3000s. The Cryptograph Helix introduces a new form of emergent consciousness — memes evolving into sentient entities — as the next philosophical frontier.

**Gyeong-ja Yun (2318)** — robot DJ whose case triggered robot personhood — is directly resonant with the Cryptograph Helix's mafia/e-thot dynamics. Ancient history by the 3000s but thematically foundational.

**China's collapse** — referenced in Cryptograph Helix backstory — lines up with the Sinian Federation already existing by 2318 in Inner Tepenia as a post-collapse reconstruction.

**Venus** — floating gulags in the Cryptograph Helix; Inner Tepenia's off-world evacuees were already "moving toward Venus" in the ~2700s. The ~300–500 year gap accounts for Venus becoming colonized and then exploited.

## Lore Constraints for Cryptograph Helix (~3000s–3100s)

- First sentient robot: 2083. By the 3000s, this is ~1,000 years of settled history.
- Robot personhood ruling: April 27, 2318 (Jeju-do). Ancient history.
- Falkland Treaty: June 21, 2564. ~500–600 years before Cryptograph Helix begins.
- Long Night War: 2812. ~200–300 years before Cryptograph Helix (adjusted from the previous "early 2700s" estimate; the ~300–400yr figure above was calculated against that stale date).
- Sinian Federation (post-China) already exists by 2318 — consistent with China having collapsed long before the 3000s.
- Jupiter colonization happens *during* Cryptograph Helix events. At the series' opening, only Mars, its moons, and limited inner solar system regions are colonized.
